Downloads

The latest version of Danger from the Deep is 0.2.0, while the simulation is very playable it is also under heavy development and is considered alpha quality but your ideas, bug reports and suggestions will shape the future of DftD and we would love to here from you even if it's just to confirm DftD works for you.

Installers

  • Windows (win32):- dangerdeep-setup-0.2.0.exe
  • GNU/Linux (32bit):- dangerdeep-0.2.0-linux-installer.bin

The above installers include both the program and the data, these are licensed under the GPL and Creative Commons respectively (see LICENSE for full details). If you do not wish to use the installers then you can download the binaries or source below but you will have to download the data/artwork separately.

Separate distributions

  • Source code:- dangerdeep-0.2.0.tar.gz
  • Artwork/data:- dangerdeep-data-0.2.0.zip
  • Win32 binaries:- dangerdeep-0.2.0-win32.zip
  • Debian unstable:- dangerdeep_0.2.0_i386.deb
  • Gentoo ebuild:- dangerdeep-0.2.0.ebuild
  • FreeBSD port:- dangerdeep-0.2.0.tbz
  • (OLD) Mandrake(10.2):- dangerdeep-0.1.0-1mdk.i586.rpm
  • (OLD) Generic RPM:- dangerdeep-0.1.0-1mdk.src.rpm

Packages marked (OLD) are looking for new maintainers, these packages are out of date and their use is discouraged.
